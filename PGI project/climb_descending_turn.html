Climb,Descending and Turns

Climb (EX7)

Objective

  • To gain altitude

Why/When

  • Take-off/ Overshoot/ Obstacle and traffic avoidance

Threshold Knowledge Test

  • How to produce nose-up attitude

Procedures

Entry:

  • Cruise attitude
  • Cockpit check L -> R
  • Scan traffic “Clear Above”
  • Landmark
  1. A: Nose up
  2. P: Full (control yaw)
  3. T: Trim

Maintain:

  1. Straight(wings level)
  2. Attitude (trim)
  3. Control yaw
  4. Check instruments (AI,ASI,Altimeter,VSI)

Recovery:

  • “100ft to go”
  • “initiate the leveloff at 50ft before the target altitude”
  1. A: Cruise
  2. P: Cruise
  3. T: Trim

Safety

  • Prolonged climb (engine heating)(oil temperature & pressure)
  • Sterile cockpit ( no talking during critical phase of flight such as landing and take-off)

Descending (EX8)

Objective

  • To lose altitude

Why/When

  • Landing/ ATC assigns lower altitude/ Engine failure (glide)

Threshold Knowledge Test

  • How to produce nose-down attitude

Procedures

Entry:

  • Cruise attitude
  • Cockpit check L -> R
  • Scan traffic “clear below”
  • Landmark
  1. P: Idle
  2. A: Nose-down (might need back pressure when the nose is down)
  3. T: Trim

Maintain:

  1. Straight (Wings level)
  2. Attitude (Trim)
  3. Control Yaw
  4. Check instruments (AI,ASI,Altimeter,VSI)

Recovery:

  • “100ft to go”
  • “initiate the leveloff at 50ft before the target altitude”
  1. P: Cruise
  2. A: Cruise
  3. T: Trim

Safety

  • Prolonged descent with low power (engine warm every 500ft)

Turns (EX9)

Objective

  • To change the heading of aircraft

Why/When

  • ATC assigns heading/ Obstacle and traffic avoidance/ Cloud avoidance

Threshold Knowledge Test

  • How to produce bank attitude

Essential Background Knowledge

  • Types of turn: Gentle(0-15), Medium(15-30), and Steep(>30)

Procedures

Entry:

  • Cruise attitude
  • Cockpit check L -> R
  • Scan traffic “clear L/R”
  • Pick 2 landmarks i.e., one in front and the other one where you want to turn
  1. Roll (move the control column in the direction of the turn)

Maintain:

  • Angle of bank: control column L/R
  • Altitude: control column back/forward
  • Airspeed: power
  • Control yaw
  • Scan tfc

Recovery:

  1. Roll wings level (when you see the landmark)

Safety

  • Scan tfc and always say “clear L/R/A/B
