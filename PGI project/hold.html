Hold

Holds Objective  To teach VOR holding patterns (hold at HUH)  To determine wind direction and apply appropriate corrections Motivation  Why/when: ① whenever an aircraft is cleared to a fix other than destination airport and delay is expected, holding instruction will be given ② poor weather ③ runway unavailable to land ④ emergency (completing checklist) Essential Background Knowledge  types of holding pattern: ① course reversal holding pattern ② arrival holding pattern to control traffic ③ missed approach holding pattern  types of holding entry: ① Direct entry ② Parallel entry: ③ Teardrop(offset) entry:  Normal holding pattern is right hand  Inbound leg must be 1min below 14,000’  Inbound leg timing is counted when the wings are level from turning  Outbound leg timing is counted when aircraft is abeam the fix Holding Clearance A holding clearance issued by ATC will include at least the following items: • a clearance to the holding fix • the direction to hold from the holding fix • a specified radial, course, or inbound track • if DME is used, the DME distances at which the fix end and outbound end turns are to be commenced (hold between [number of miles] and [number of miles]). If the outbound DME is not specified by ATC, pilots are expected adhere to the standard holding pattern timing procedures above • the altitude or FL to be maintained • the time to expect further clearance or an approach clearance or the time to leave the fix in the event of a communications failure Procedures - Standard holding pattern - Holding pattern thumb method to find out the entry type: (Reference video: https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=YbIw6kdytnU) - Holding entry procedures ① Sector 1 (parallel entry):  2 left turns & 1 right turn  1st left turn should be the outbound heading after the fix and fly 1 min outbound leg  2nd left turn should be to intercept the inbound radial  Right turn should be performed when the flag changes to “from” ② Sector 2 (teardrop entry):  Upon reaching the fix, turn 30˚ towards parallel side and fly 1 min leg on that heading  Upon reaching 1min, turn right to intercept inbound radial ③ Sector 3 (Direct entry):  Upon reaching the fix, turn right to outbound heading and fly the holding pattern Safety - Holding speeds - Holding endurance
