Flight Items PPL

Youtube Video: Anna’s flight test

  1. Pre-flight inspection:

    • student will be asked one or two questions regarding aircraft handling during the pre-flight inspection
    • check compass card the day before and complete deviation on the navigation log accordingly
    • put a hood in the aircraft

  2. Departure & Enroute Procedures

    (i.e., ETA, 5T’s, position report, ground speed check)

  3. Instrument flying & unusual attitude recovery

  4. Upper airwork:

    • Steep Turn
    • Slow flight
    • Poweroff & Poweron Stall
    • Spiral dive
    • Slipping

  5. Lower airwork:

    • Forced approach
    • Precautionary landing
    • Diversion

  6. Circuit:

    • landing briefing
      • types of landing
      • runway number
      • approach speed and flap setting
      • touch down point
    • Short field Takeoff and landing
    • Soft field takeoff and landing
    • Normal takeoff and landing

  7. Emergency:

    • Student will be asked 2 emergencies from one of the follwing items below either on the ground or in the air


    1. partial power loss
    2. rough engine operation or overheat
    3. loss of oil pressure
    4. fuel starvation
    5. electrical fire
    6. vacuum system failure
    7. pitot or static blockage
    8. cabin fire
    9. carb icing
    10. electrical malfunctions
    11. landing gear malfunctions
    12. brake failure or seizure
    13. flap failure
    14. door opening in flight
    15. spin recovery
    16. emergency descent
    17. any other emergency unique to the type of aeroplane flown
