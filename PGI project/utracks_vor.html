U-Tracks and VOR

Objective - To practice instrument scanning and situational awareness - To familiarize with the takeoff profile (climb, descents, and GUMPS) - To set up the VOR for radio navigation and practice VOR intercepts - To practice clearance copying and making standard calls Motivation - Basic skill in instrument flying Threshold Knowledge Test - Level GUMPS - Pre-landing GUMPS - VOR check on the ground Essential Background Knowledge - How to find a position from VOR - How to track IB/OB to the station - How to intercept and track IB/OB on a specific radial - Primary instruments & secondary instruments - IFR Phraseology (https://www.navcanada.ca/EN/media/Publications/NAV_19-108_Phraseology_IFR-E6_WEB.pdf) Procedures - Making U track with 1min for each leg (also get familiar with 5Ts) - VOR interceptions Safety
