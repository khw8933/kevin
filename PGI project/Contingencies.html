Procedures

  1. Lost Communications
    • Aviate (keep flying)
    • Navigate (flying circuits until downwind)
    • Investigate (cause check)
      1. Frequency
      2. Radio switch & volume check
      3. Headset check
      4. Avionics switch
      5. Circuit breaker check
      6. Stuck mic (T sign when mic switch is keyed/ say intention to tower and turn off the radio)
    • Communicate
      1. Squwak 7600
      2. Check light signal from the tower
  2. Runway Change in Controlled aerodrome / Uncontrolled aerodrome
    • Tower will instruct in a controlled aerodrome
    • Extend downwind and cross midfield in an uncontrolled aerodrome
  3. Spacing
    • Traffic numbers
    • Slow down
    • Right of way
    • Base turn (wing abeam other aircraft or ahead aircraft turn final)
  4. Bounce & High flare recovery

      5. Bounce

    • Cause: landing too fast & landing too hard
    • Recovery: overshoot

      • High flare

    • Cause: flare too early
    • Recovery: overshoot
  5. Overshoot
    • Aviate
      1. Power up (full)
      2. Nose up (on the horizon)
      3. Flaps up (in stages)
    • Navigate
    • Communicate
  6. Engine failure (TKT crew briefing)
    • On the runway
    • Right after the takeoff
    • Circuit height
