EX24 Radio Aids - ADF Objective To teach:  How to use ADF to find a position  To track IB/OB  To intercept IB/OB  To find a position using 2 NDBs Motivation  Why: - Cross Country - Licensing requirement Threshold Knowledge Test Essential background Knowledge  Equipment on the ground: NDB station (small dotted circles on the chart) - Data box for NDB (EX. Name: White Rock NDB, Identifier: WC (.-- -.-.), Frequency: 332 KHz ) - No radial (only track)  Equipment in the aircraft: - ADF antenna on the bottom of the aircraft usually (Fixed card relative bearing indicator & ADF receiver) - Frequency selection & volume control - Fixed card ADF (North is always pointing top) Procedures  Always do this step before using ADF: - Tune(frequency) – Identify(mores code) – Set(MC with HI) 1. Find a position ① T-I-S (HI/MC) ② BFS = MH + Tail of the needle EX) your heading is 040 and if the tail of the ADF needle is on 050 = you are on 090 track 2. Track OB ① Find a position ② Turn to MH + tail of the needle ③ Confirm 3. Track home inbound ① T-I-S ② Turn to MH + head of the needle ③ Confirm 4. Intercept OB (Ex. intercept and track OB 090 track at 90°) ① T-I-S ② Turn parallel (Ex. 090) ③ Check head needle: Right = turn right / Left = turn left ④ Turn heading 090 +090 = 180° ⑤ Intercept head needle at 90° ⑥ Turn to 090 5. Intercept IB (EX. Intercept and track IB 270 track at 90°) ① T-I-S ② Turn parallel (EX. 270) ③ Check head needle: Right = turn right / Left = turn left ④ Turn heading 270 – 090 = 180° ⑤ Intercept head needle at 90° ⑥ Turn to 270 6. Position fix using 2 NDBs ① Same position finding procedure twice Safety  T-I-S is a must
