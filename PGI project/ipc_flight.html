Tips

  • Don't forget to press the OBS button before the hold
  • Don't forget the hold briefing including speed, altitude, and endurance to hold
  • For single engine, remove drag (ex., if it is a straigh in approach, gears stay and flaps up. If it is a circling approach, flaps stay and gears up)
