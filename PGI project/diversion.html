Diversion

Why/When?

  • Deteriorating weather on intended route
  • Fuel shortage
  • Mechanical problems

Procedures

    Things to be completed before reaching the SHP

  1. Planning
    • Circle (SHP)
    • Circle (destination)
    • Line
    • Heading
    • Distance
    • Time = 2/3 of distance
    • Fuel = 1/10 of time
  2. Cruise Check
    • Set heading indicator with Magnetic compass

    Upon reaching the SHP

  3. Perform 5Tâ€™s
    • Turn: planned heading
    • Time: take time and calculate ETA to destination
    • Throttle: cruise power (2300rpm)
    • Tune: FSS or nearby ATC
    • Talk: inform diversion and ETA
  4. Ground Speed Check
    • Inform ground speed check point ( Is it half of the way? 1/3 of the way? 1/4 of the way?)
    • Upon reaching ground speed check point, check how long it took from SHP
    • Calculate RETA based on the time difference from SHP to ground speed check point
    • If RETA is more than 15min from ETA, inform FSS or ATC
