Weather Minimums:


  1. No clouds below 10000 feet or 1000 feet above safe cruising altitude, whichever is higher.
  2. No fog in valleys.
  3. Visibility at or better than 10 SM.
  4. Winds not more than 20 Knots.

Procedures:


CZBB – Pitt Lake

Pitt Lake - CYSE

  • From Pitt Lake, start climbing to 8500’ or higher (caution limited oxygen above 10,000’)
  • Contact Vancouver Terminal 125.2 above 8500’ and request flight following to Squamish
  • Select Top of Descent Point where you start descending to join (circuit height + 500’) to check active runway at CYSE (ex., after Pinecone Lake, start descending to 1700’ until CYSE)
  • Cross midfield and check the windsock, if active runway is found, loose altitude to circuit height in upwind side
  • Make 5 uncontrolled airport mandatory calls!

CYSE – CYPS

  • Climb to 3500’ following river or highway to CYPS
  • When departing from CYSE, mind the noise sensitive area
  • Select Top of Descent point to descend until circuit height +500’. (i.e., 2200’)
  • Cross midfield and check the windsock, if active runway is found, loose altitude to circuit height in upwind side
  • Make 5 uncontrolled airport mandatory calls!

CYPS – Harrison Hot Springs

  • Altitude at discretion (3500’, 5500’, or 7500’)
  • Follow the Lillooet Lake or highway until Harrison Hot Springs
