ILS Approach

Objective

  • To learn how to perform ILS approach

Threshold Knowledge Test

  • Precision approach:
    • A precision approach is an instrument approach and landing using precision lateral and vertical guidance with minima as determined by the category of operation
  • MDA/DA
  • MAP(missed approach point) for precision approach:
    • The MAP on a precision approach is reached when the aircraft reaches the decision height prescribed for the approach while maintaining the glideslope

Essential Background Knowledge

  • Instrument Landing System (ILS) is defined as a precision runway approach aid based on two radio beams which together provide pilots with both vertical and horizontal guidance during an approach to land
